Notation review

  • A, B, C -- propositions
  • A ∧ B -- conjunction ("and")
  • A ∨ B -- disjunction ("or")
  • A → B -- implication ("if A then B")
  • A ↔ B -- bi-implication ("A iff B")
  • ¬ A -- negation ("not A")
  • ⊥ -- false, falsum, nonsense, bullshit, the middle finger
